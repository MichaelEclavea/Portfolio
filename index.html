About

Firstly, becoming a software developer has always been a long time dream of mine. Taking this step to actually becoming a developer, is one of my proudest moments I have of myself.

As I have done in every previous job I have had, I take my work serious and producing excellent work is what I aim for everyday. To help me accomplish this, I have completed a rigorous boot-camp at CodeFellows while simultaneously completing other certificates in the process. I also produced personal projects outside each curriculum utilizing strictly JavaScript. As I continued learning new languages and frameworks, I re-wrote each personal project I had to better understand what I was learning at the time. This created a lot more work for myself throughout the whole bootcamp process, but I felt it was needed for a deeper understanding and to give me a more repetitive hands on approach.

I am self motivated and highly self driven. I am not only continually trying to improve my current skills as a developer, but I also created a habit to read books on how to be a better dad, a better partner and to overall be a better person.

Whatever position I am in, despite the field, I always put it in more than expected of me. I help anyone around me, no matter their position or job title or even if it means it will be more work put on myself. I enjoy helping people around me and contributing more to anything I touch.
